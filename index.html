About Me

Discover my journey in voice acting and creative arts

I'm a passionate voice actor and creative artist with over 8 years of professional experience bringing characters and stories to life.

My journey began in university theater, where I discovered my love for character work and storytelling. Since then, I've worked on over 500 projects ranging from anime dubbing to commercial campaigns, each one teaching me something new about the craft.

What sets me apart is my versatility – I can seamlessly transition from a heroic protagonist to a quirky sidekick, or from an authoritative narrator to a playful character. My background in digital art also brings a unique visual perspective to audio work.
